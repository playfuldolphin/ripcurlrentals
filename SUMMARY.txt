NEXT STEPS (IN ORDER)
================================================================================

1. ADD YOUR PHOTOS
   - Edit: src/data/properties.js
   - Replace image URLs with your property photos
   - Upload to Imgur (free) or use your own hosting

2. UPDATE PROPERTY INFO
   - Edit: src/data/properties.js
   - Change names, descriptions, prices, amenities
   - Update bedroom/bathroom counts

3. SET CONTACT INFO
   - Edit: src/components/Footer.jsx
   - Edit: src/pages/Home.jsx
   - Edit: src/pages/Property.jsx (booking form email)
   - Replace bookings@riptiderentals.com with YOUR email
   - Replace (904) 555-0123 with YOUR phone

4. TEST LOCALLY
   - Visit http://localhost:5173
   - Click through all pages
   - Test booking form
   - Check on mobile

5. DEPLOY TO WEB
   - Push to GitHub
   - Deploy on Netlify (free) or Vercel (free)
   - Get a custom domain (optional, ~$12/year)
